Mushroom Bone Hunt

This project was made by a small team of 3 designers, and was fit to a client brief. The goal was to make a game where the mechanics revolved around physics, and so Mushroom Bone Hunt was born. The player is a mushroom who finds a disassembled skeleton and must solve basic physics puzzles to obtain the missing bones and reassemble the skeleton.
